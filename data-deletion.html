Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data associated with our app, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Send an email to support@yourdomain.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. In the body of the email, please include your user ID and any other relevant information.
  3. Our support team will process your request within 7 business days and confirm the deletion of your data.

If you have any questions, please contact our support team at support@yourdomain.com.
